Explore Doughp’s delicious and safe-to-eat edible cookie dough flavors. Made for snacking or baking, each flavor brings joy with every scoop!

Doughp’s Best-Selling Flavors

At Doughp, we believe that cookie dough should be enjoyed safely, and that’s why we’ve crafted a lineup of egg-free, edible cookie dough flavors made for everyone. Our cookie dough is perfect for scooping straight from the jar or baking into warm, fresh cookies.

Our best-selling flavors include classics like chocolate chip, peanut butter, and birthday cake, all made with the finest ingredients for a premium taste. For those looking to try something new, Doughp offers limited-edition seasonal flavors that bring a unique twist to your favorite dessert.

The Safe and Tasty Ingredients We Use

We’ve taken care to ensure that each batch of Doughp is crafted with high-quality, safe ingredients. Our dough is free from eggs, so there’s no need to worry about raw consumption – just enjoy every scoop! And if you’re in the mood to bake, Doughp can easily be transformed into cookies with just a quick bake in the oven. The options are endless, and the flavor is unmatched.

How to Enjoy Doughp: Snacking & Baking Tips

Whether you’re a cookie dough purist who loves to eat it raw or a baking enthusiast looking to experiment, Doughp has you covered. Treat yourself to a jar of happiness and experience the unique flavors and textures of Doughp’s edible cookie dough today.
